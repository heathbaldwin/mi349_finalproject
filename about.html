About Us

Heath's Hoodies was founded by Heath Baldwin who is currently a student at Michigan State University and lives in East Lansing, Michigan. The company was originally started during the COVID-19 pandemic when Heath had alot of freetime on his hands and decided to start a small business making custom embroidered hoodies and clothing items. Heath first bought a small custom emvroidery machine and then scaled his business to the point where we needed to purchase a much larger and expensive Brother-1900 machine. Heath Hoodies now is hoping to soon fully transition themselves from Etsy to their own custom designed site in the next couple months and this is the first step. Enjoy the site!
